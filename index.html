Enhance music practice through randomization

About
Welcome to the Scale Scrambler!

This application allows musicians to use randomization to supplement fundamentals, improving technical consistency and flexibility.

To get started, choose the keys and exercises you would like to work on. Exercises will be randomized within each selected key - this will help you think in keys rather than in the specific order of a warmup routine.

Your settings are saved in your browser, so you will only need to customize everything once!

Written by Debashis Biswas of the Arizona State University Clarinet Studio
